WHAT THIS IS

A space you can
actually walk into.

.01Most listings show you photos. Simax scans the real space and rebuilds it as a fully walkable 3D environment — you move through it with WASD on desktop or a thumb joystick on mobile, look around freely, and stop at hotspots for details.

.02No app download, no headset. It runs straight in the browser, tuned to stay smooth even on a mid-range phone — because that's where most of your visitors actually are.

HOW IT WORKS

Built for real phones,
not just demo laptops.

Real-world scan

The physical space is captured and turned into an accurate 3D model — real dimensions, real layout.

Adaptive rendering

Shadows are pre-computed once, not every frame, and heavy effects auto-drop on mobile — so it stays smooth instead of draining a battery.

Real collision

Walls and furniture actually stop you — no clipping through the counter, no floating through the wall.

Runs in the browser

One link. No app store, no install, works the moment someone taps it from a text or a QR code.
